Motivation


How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

I feel it's a great way to live life if you are able to sustain motivation by living through the purpose lens. Finding the motivation each day to do something isn't always easy, and if you don't love what you are doing it usually doesn't lead to happiness and in time you just become even more demotivated and unhappy. Sure you work and count down the days until your next holiday which only really gives you happiness in a short burst before you go back to daily life and look for the next high milestone to work towards. That kind of motivation is short term and feels so forced. I find that people who are most happy and energised each day about their life are those who strive or have a purpose that is higher than themselves. This is something I've always observed but this week's reading really explained why this way of living will lead to more sustained motivation and happiness. I guess it's beneficail to have a DMN to figure out how you can live your life through the purpose lens and be more motivated and happy.
